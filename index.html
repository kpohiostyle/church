"Infuriating Unfairness"

by Elder Dale G. Renlund

Saturday Afternoon Session

General Questions

  • What is unfair?
  • Is unfair only personal?
  • Can we experience unfairness for others?
  • What causes unfairness?

Talking Points

Examples of unfairness: A child that gets a weekly allowance. One child spends its on sweets. The other child saves up for a new bike. First child thinks its "unfair" that their sibling received a new bike. What does this teach us?

When others reveive benefits because of their diligent choices, we cannot rightly conclude that we have been treated unfairly when we have had the same opportunity

Other example: My brother-in-law and sister-in-law had a baby girl a few years back. Her name was Maggie. Maggie was less than 24hrs old when she was taken from them. Ruled Sudden Infant death syndrome. My words cannot even begin to convey the kind of hardship that must have been for them. Even I felt the pain of that, I questioned why did this happen, how did this happen. How could you do this to them? Unfair came to mind often. How they survived is beyond me, but through it all they never lost faith. Ever year for her birthday they ask us as families to go to the temple.
